---
title: Getting Started
description: Spin up a workspace, create a project, and start collecting feedback
---

1. **Sign Up & Create a Workspace**
   Get started by signing up and creating a workspace (your organization). This serves as the central hub for managing projects and boards.
2. **Create a Project**
   Within your workspace, set up projects to track feedback for specific products or services. Each project has its own board.
3. **Customize Your Board**
   Set the project's visibility (public or private), customize its statuses, and add tags to organize posts effectively.
4. **Invite Your Users**
   Share your board with users, customers, or internal teams. Encourage them to submit posts and vote on existing ideas.
5. **Review & Prioritize**
   Use Backfeed's prioritization tools to filter, sort, and rank posts based on importance and feasibility.
6. **Plan & Execute**
   Move posts through your statuses to build the roadmap, hand accepted work to [Runa](/core/runa), and close the loop with reporters when it ships.
