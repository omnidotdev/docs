---
title: Statuses, Roadmap & Changelog
description: Tracking and communicating progress back to the people who asked
---

Statuses, the roadmap, and the changelog are one connected system: move a post
through a status and the roadmap and changelog follow, and the people who asked
get notified.

## Statuses & timeline

Fully customizable per-project statuses (e.g. Under Review, Planned, In Progress,
Completed). Each post carries a status-change **timeline**: who moved it where,
and when, with an optional note on each change.

## Roadmap

A read-only board that groups posts by status to communicate what you are
building, separating public communication from internal planning.

## Changelog

An opt-in, public, read-only feed of shipped posts, per project.

## Status notifications

When a post changes status, the people who reported or upvoted it are emailed so
the loop closes. Each person can opt out in their notification preferences.

## Metrics

Each project surfaces at-a-glance metrics: post counts, total engagement,
contributor activity, and a status breakdown.
