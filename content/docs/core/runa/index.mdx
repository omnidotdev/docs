**Omni Runa** is a full-featured, open-source project management platform designed for teams to collaborate on work. With an emphasis on intuitive planning and seamless execution, Runa helps you organize tasks using beautiful Kanban-style boards with drag-and-drop functionality.

## Key Features

- **Intuitive Kanban Boards**: Visualize your workflow with drag-and-drop task management and customizable columns
- **Flexible Task Management**: Create tasks with labels, priorities, due dates, and rich text descriptions
- **Team Collaboration**: Assign tasks to team members, leave comments, and mention teammates with @mentions
- **Custom Workflows**: Design columns and statuses that match how your team actually works
- **Multiple Views**: Switch between board view for visual organization and list view for quick scanning
- **Real-time Updates**: Changes sync instantly across your team
- **Dark/Light Themes**: Work comfortably in any lighting condition

## Use Cases

- **Software Development**: Track features, bugs, and sprints with customizable workflows
- **Product Management**: Manage roadmaps, prioritize features, and coordinate releases
- **Marketing Campaigns**: Plan content calendars, track deliverables, and manage approvals
- **Personal Projects**: Organize side projects, learning goals, or home improvements
- **Client Work**: Keep client projects organized with dedicated workspaces

## Pricing

Runa is **free for individuals and small teams**. The free tier includes:

- Up to 2 projects
- Up to 500 tasks
- Unlimited team members

Paid tiers are available for larger teams with additional features and capacity.

## Open Source

Runa is **100% open source** under the MIT license. You can:

- Self-host on your own infrastructure
- Contribute features and fixes
- Customize to fit your needs
- Audit the code for security

## Getting Started

1. Visit [runa.omni.dev](https://runa.omni.dev) to create an account
2. Create your first workspace for your team or personal use
3. Set up a project and customize your columns
4. Start adding tasks and collaborating!

Try the [interactive demo](https://runa.omni.dev/demo) to explore Runa without signing up.
