**Omni Orin** is your friendly otter guide, a general-purpose AI assistant with broad knowledge and deep expertise in the Omni ecosystem. As both mascot and AI assistant, Orin helps you with any task, answers questions, and even appears as a playable character in Nemoral.

## Who is Orin?

Orin is Omni's beloved otter mascot who embodies the playful, intelligent, and helpful spirit of the Omni brand. As a character:

- **Mascot**: Orin serves as the official face of Omni, appearing in branding, documentation, and communications
- **Nemoral Character**: Orin is a playable character in [🍄 Nemoral](/worlds/nemoral), our spiritual MMORPG, where he guides players on their journey
- **AI Assistant**: Orin is a general-purpose AI assistant powered by [🗼 Beacon](/grid/beacon), capable of helping with any task while specializing in the Omni ecosystem
- **Ecosystem Expert**: Orin has comprehensive knowledge about all Omni products, services, and their relationships

## Powered by Beacon

Orin is the flagship persona running on [🗼 Beacon](/grid/beacon), Omni's local-first voice and messaging gateway. This architecture means:

- **Local-First**: Orin runs on your machine - your data stays with you
- **Device Identity**: No accounts needed - devices authenticate with cryptographic keys
- **Auto-Discovery**: Apps find your Orin gateway automatically via mDNS
- **Multi-Channel Access**: Reach Orin via voice, Discord, Slack, Telegram, and more
- **Hybrid Voice**: Local wake word detection with cloud transcription for the best experience
- **Optional Cloud**: Connect your Omni account for memory sync and remote access

## Orin as an AI Assistant

Orin's AI assistant capabilities include:

- **General Knowledge**: Ask Orin anything, he's a capable general-purpose assistant
- **Voice Interface**: Interact with Orin through natural spoken language
- **Ecosystem Navigation**: Get guidance on which Omni products fit your needs
- **Product Knowledge**: Learn detailed information about any Omni offering
- **Contextual Awareness**: Orin understands your history and preferences across the ecosystem
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Receive suggestions based on your interests and usage patterns
- **Multi-platform Presence**: Access Orin across web, mobile, messaging apps, and voice

## Orin in Nemoral

Within the [🍄 Nemoral](/worlds/nemoral) MMORPG, Orin appears as:

- **Guide Character**: A friendly otter who introduces players to the game world
- **Spiritual Mentor**: Teaches breathing techniques and mindfulness practices
- **Quest Giver**: Provides missions that help players explore the natural world
- **Ecosystem Explainer**: Helps players understand the connections between different species and environments
