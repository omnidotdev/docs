The dashboard at [charm.omni.dev](https://charm.omni.dev) is a point-and-click
alternative to the in-Discord slash commands. Everything you can set with a
command, you can set here, with a live preview of your welcome card as you edit.

## Sign in

1. Open [charm.omni.dev](https://charm.omni.dev) and choose **Sign in**. You
   authenticate with your Omni account.
2. The first time, you connect your Discord account so Charm knows which servers
   you manage. Only servers where you are the owner or have the **Administrator**
   permission appear.

## Pick a server

The **Your servers** page lists every server you administer that has Charm added.
Select one to open its configuration. Do not see a server? Make sure Charm has
been [invited](/kindred/charm/getting-started) to it and that you have the right
permissions.

## Configure

Each server has a section per feature, matching the slash commands:

- **Welcome** turns join and leave messages on or off, sets their channels and
  text, and toggles the image card. The card preview updates as you type.
- **Moderation** sets the mod-log channel and whether actioned members are
  notified by DM.
- **Roles** manages autoroles (assigned on join) and reaction roles.
- **Logging** sends message and member event logs to a channel.
- **Automod** configures the content filters and anti-raid protection.
- **Leveling** enables XP, sets the announcement channel and cooldown, and maps
  levels to reward roles.
- **Theme** picks a preset or custom colors and your embed branding.

Changes save per section. See [Features](/kindred/charm/features) for what each
setting does, or configure the same options with
[slash commands](/kindred/charm/getting-started) if you prefer to stay in Discord.
